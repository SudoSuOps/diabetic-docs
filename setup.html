Getting started

Run the bee on your Mac

Get the diabetic-smart helper running on your own machine in a few minutes — free, private, and offline. Nothing leaves your house. We just help you carry the day. 🐝

What you'll need

A Mac (Mac Mini, MacBook, iMac) with about 8 GB+ of memory and 3 GB of free disk. The compact DiabeticDaily-4B model is a ~2.7 GB download and runs comfortably on a Mac Mini. Pick either path below — both keep everything 100% on your machine.

Option A — Ollama (quickest, one command)

  1. Download Ollama from ollama.com and drag it into your Applications folder.
  2. Open Terminal (press ⌘-Space, type "Terminal"), paste this line, and press Return:
ollama run hf.co/SwarmandBee/DiabeticDaily-4B:Q4_K_M
  1. It downloads the model once (~2.7 GB), then you can talk to the bee right in the terminal. Try: "Give me a quick win for today." Type /bye to exit.

Option B — LM Studio (no terminal, point & click)

  1. Download LM Studio from lmstudio.ai and open it.
  2. In the search box, type SwarmandBee DiabeticDaily-4B and download the Q4_K_M GGUF file.
  3. Load the model and start chatting in the app. That's it — a private bee, running on your Mac.

Got a bigger Mac?

The same family comes in three sizes — the bigger the machine, the bigger the brain. DiabeticDaily-9B and DiabeticDaily-27B are on Hugging Face too. Browse them all at huggingface.co/SwarmandBee.

What this is — and what's coming

✅ What you get today: the bee's brain — a diabetic-smart helper that educates and organizes, running entirely on your Mac. No cloud, no account, no data leaving your house.

🛠️ What we're still building: the full LocalDiabetic box — your records, reminders, supplies and care notes wired into the helper with a simple dashboard. That part isn't a one-click download yet, and we're not in the app store yet. We won't pretend otherwise.
