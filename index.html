The single source of truth

DiabeticDocs is the single source of truth for the OpenDiabetic ecosystem. Not marketing — the reproducible record. It covers both halves: (1) what the system is (the vault, the firewall, the edge brain), and (2) how dev teams actually use our deeded datasets and crockpot-cook diabetic models — the why, the how, the doctrine.

We are open-source. Everything here is built to be verified and vetted by anyone: cited datasets, SHA-256-checked downloads, reproducible recipes, deeded receipts. LIVE = running & verified · IN BUILD = partly done · FRAMEWORK = designed, not yet a service. No vaporware.

The one law

Private health data (PHI) never leaves your box. Models flow down, receipts flow up, PHI crosses never. The vault holds your records; the cloud only ever receives non-PHI receipts, open models, and templates. → The Firewall

How it all connects

One cited-fact spine runs through everything: DiabeticTruth publishes verifiable facts → DiabeticDatasets turns them into deeded training corpora → Diabetic Crockpot Cooks slow-cooks them into models on OpenDiabetic compute → the models flow down to the LocalDiabetic edge brain on a diabetic's own box → and never does PHI flow back up, only receipts. The whole loop is doctrine-governed and verifiable end to end.
