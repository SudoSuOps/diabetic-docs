OpenDiabetic is a slow cooker

Why? Because lives depend on us. Truth over compromise.
A diabetic reads this model's output and makes a real decision about their own body. The cost of a wrong answer isn't a bad demo — it's a person. So we cook slow, deliberate, verified, and deeded. Nothing ships fast at the expense of being right.

The founder is the user

OpenDiabetic isn't built for diabetics from the outside. It's built by one — living the full continuum: type 1, lost toes, more than a dozen foot surgeries, the whole road. The product is the help the founder needed and couldn't get: "without a swarm of help, how would I get there?" That's why the model is the swarm — and why it has to be honest. We are our own first user.

Truth over compromise — what it actually costs us

Being a slow cooker is a set of deliberate refusals. Each one slows us down on purpose:

We refuse to……because
Publish a fact without a citationA diabetic can't act on "trust me." No source, no publish. → DiabeticTruth
Ship a dataset you can't verify yourselfCrystal-clear or it isn't open. Schema, samples, size, SHA-256 inline. → Datasets
Call a model "good" without beating baseBeat-base-or-kill. A held-out A/B, deterministic gates, or it doesn't ship. → Doctrine
Cut a cook short to save time or moneyWe own the rigs and the electrons. Quality is the only axis. → Crockpot Cook
Let private health data leave the boxThe one law. PHI crosses never. → The Firewall
Work in a black boxThe "AI trust-me-bro" era is over. Every cook is a flightsheet anyone can audit.

The model: give people what they need, not what you have

The human side of OpenDiabetic costs the people it serves nothing — no charge, no strings. A ride to the doctor. Diabetic shoes shipped. The continuum of care. Funded by recycling sovereign-compute income back into the people. Dignity, not charity. Some of that continuum is LIVE (reminders, organization); some is still FRAMEWORK (the ride network, the shoe-shipping) — and we label which is which, always. Never vaporware.

Why this lives in the docs

The entire ecosystem lives in these docs because open-source means verifiable by anyone. The why isn't decoration — it's the reason every page that follows is built the way it is. If you're going to crockpot-cook on this platform, start here: the discipline only makes sense once you understand the stakes.
